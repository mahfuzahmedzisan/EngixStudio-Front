Our story

From Vision to Reality: The Journey of EngixStudio

Meet Robin Rabbi, the visionary founder behind EngixStudio. With a passion for design and innovation, Robin embarked on a mission to create a studio where creativity meets functionality, bringing impactful digital solutions to life.

It all started with a belief that great design has the power to transform businesses. In 2024, Robin founded EngixStudio with the aim to deliver design solutions that not only look great but also drive meaningful results for clients across industries.

Before establishing EngixStudio, Robin gained years of experience working with global brands and startups, honing his expertise in design and development. Today, EngixStudio stands as a trusted partner for businesses, offering unparalleled design solutions and empowering them to achieve new heights.
